Selected Project

AI-Assisted 3D Reconstruction of Graphene

Developed a novel AI-driven framework for reconstructing the 3D atomic structure of free-standing graphene using low-dose TEM imaging. Integrated Simulated Annealing with Molecular Dynamics to ensure physically valid configurations under noise-limited conditions.

Achieved sub-angstrom accuracy in 3D structure recovery and successfully captured the dynamic rippling behavior of graphene induced by electron stimulation. Results provide new insight into how 3D atomic distortions influence electronic properties.
